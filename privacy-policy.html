Privacy Policy

Hex VR Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 06, 2024

Welcome to Hex VR, developed by Golden Gear Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about users of our virtual reality game, Hex VR ("the App").

Data Collection

  • User Data: The App collects meta username data, which is used solely for the purpose of creating and maintaining a username system within the game.
  • Microphone Usage: In certain online features of the App, such as multiplayer modes or voice chat, the App may access your device's microphone for communication purposes. This access is explicitly for facilitating in-game communication and is not used for any other purpose.

How App Uses Data

  • Username System: The meta username data collected is used to create and display user identities within the App. This enhances the multiplayer experience by allowing users to identify each other during gameplay.
  • Microphone Usage: The microphone access is used only during online features where communication between players is enabled. It allows for real-time voice communication within the game environment to enhance collaboration and social interaction among players.

Data Removal Requests

Users who wish to request the removal of their data from our systems may do so by contacting us at hex.reporting@gmail.com. Please provide your username or any relevant information for us to locate and remove your data from our servers. Please note that certain data may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

For any inquiries or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your data, please contact us at hex.reporting@gmail.com.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at hexvrproject@gmail.com.

Thank you for using Hex VR!
