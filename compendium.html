Hex VR

Hex VR is a multiplayer team based sports game in VR

Surrounded by a blend of myth and magic, legends aren't born. They're forged.

Champions

Hex VR's Primary Gamemode

The Stadium is home to the sport of Champions. Two teams of 3 face off attempting to place the disk in the opposing team's goal while defending their own.

Knights

Knights are elite Champions athletes in Hex VR.

Whether they're after fame, fortune, recognition, or just the thrill of the game, Knights are the best of the best.

Underground

Hex VR's Secondary Gamemode

The Underground is home to a mixed bag of scoundrels, criminals, and people just down on their luck trying to survive. Two teams of 5 face off in gladiatorial combat. This is a death game where anything goes.

Thugs

Thugs are the dangerous participants of the Underground.

Assassins, mercenaries, murderers, and the criminally insane. All killers are welcome. No matter their background, participants are only in it for the thrill of the kill, the cash prize, or both.

Nexus Crystals

Nexus Crystals are magical energy sources.

Used by mages in centuries past, these crystals now power limited methods of magic like Rune Weaving.

Rune Weaving

Rune Weaving is a method to harness Nexus Energy.

Through decades of research, the lost art of Rune Weaving has returned, enabling feats like levitation and teleportation.

Swift Gates

Swift Gates launch players at high speeds.

Charged with magical energy, these gates propel players in specific directions, found in both the Champions Stadium and the Underground tunnels.

Pedestals

Pedestals are the player's main interface.

These multipurpose sources of information are essential for interacting with the game's world and mechanics.
